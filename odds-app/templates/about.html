About This App

What is GoldDerby?

GoldDerby is a platform that allows users to predict the winners of various entertainment awards, including the Oscars, Emmys, and Grammys. It features predictions from a variety of groups: industry experts, top-performing users, and its broader user base.

The Three Groups of GoldDerby Voters

  • Experts: Hollywood industry journalists and insiders who are well-versed in awards season trends and likely outcomes.
  • All Star 24 Users: A group of the 24 users with the best record of predicting Oscar winners over the last two years. These users are among the most reliable predictors on the platform.
  • All GoldDerby Users: The general user base, consisting of anyone who has signed up for an account on GoldDerby. This group provides a wider range of perspectives.

Betting Odds

This app also incorporates betting odds sourced from the website OddsChecker. These odds are determined by betting markets, which can reflect broader public sentiment as well as professional insights.

Key Features

  • Movie Odds: A page that compares betting odds to GoldDerby’s own odds (calculated using an unclear methodology). This provides an interesting view of how betting markets diverge from GoldDerby’s predictions.
  • Betting Odds vs. All Star 24 Users: A page that compares betting odds with the percentage of votes received by each movie from GoldDerby’s All Star 24 Users. This feature can be particularly useful, as it leverages predictions from a group of individuals with a proven track record of accurately forecasting awards outcomes.
